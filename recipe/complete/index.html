Matcha Lemonade Recipe

[recipe]

What You Need

  1. Matcha
  2. Mason Jar
  3. Teaspoon
  1. Water
  2. Lemonade
  3. Ice

How To Make It: (one serving, ~5 minutes)

  1. Prepare your ingredients
  2. pour 6 ounces of water into your jar
  3. pour 6 ounces of lemonade into your jar
  4. add 1 to 2 tablespoons of matcha into the mixture
  5. add ice and shake well (for about 20 sec.)
  6. Done! (drink in a timely manner, matcha does not keep for long in the fridge)
