Matcha Lemonade Recipe

What You Need

• Mason Jar

• Teaspoon

• Ice

• Water

• Lemonade

• Matcha Powder

How To Make It (one serving, 5 minutes)

1. Prepare your ingredients

2. Pour 6 ounces of water into your jar

3. Pour 6 ounces of lemonade into your jar

4. Add 1 to 2 tablespoons of matcha powder to the mixture

5. Add ice and shake

6. Done! (drink in timely manner, matcha does not keep well in fridge)
